As a college student taking web development subject, we find that photography may help us to cope with the stress and challenges we are facing right now, particularly during this pandemic. Things like taking photos and appreciating the beauty of nature itself is one of the best things you can do while you are alive.

Nostalgic, real, raw, and relaxing are words that come to mind when describing our photography. Images that allow you to experience the in-between moments while appreciating the beauty of nature. Because our photography isn't only about capturing the image; it's also about capturing the moments along the way.
